As part of the Textile/Surface Design program, we have to learn a whole variety of handmade techniques for developing textile prints besides the computer aid produced. When I started my sophomore year in 2014, I had my first Screen Printing Class. At the beginning I didn't like it. At the end of the semester I learnt to manage it. I wasn't until my second screen printing class that I started to love it. I got to have some decent work.

Last semester, I was really excited to finally have Scarves Screen Printing. I had the opportunity to work with 3 big size screens instead of 2. This gave me the chance to have 6 colors in my print due to the color trapping. I also spend more money to work on silk which was an excellent fabric for scarves.
